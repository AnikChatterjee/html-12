Birbhum district is an administrative unit in the Indian state of West Bengal.
It is the northernmost district of Burdwan division—one of the five administrative divisions of West Bengal.
The district headquarters is in Suri. Other important cities are Rampurhat, Bolpur and Sainthia.
Area: 4,545 km²
Population: 35 lakhs (2011)
