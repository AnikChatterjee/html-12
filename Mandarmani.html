Mandarmani is a seaside resort village in the state of West Bengal, India, and lies in East Midnapore district, at the northern end of the Bay of Bengal.
It is one of the largest seaside resorts of West Bengal, fast-developing
Area : 24.36 km²
Population : 12,307 (2011)
